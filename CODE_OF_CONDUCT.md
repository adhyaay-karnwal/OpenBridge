# Code of Conduct

OpenBridge contributors are expected to keep project spaces focused, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about technical concerns.
- Keep discussions tied to the project and its users.
- Give maintainers enough context to reproduce issues and review changes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or exploit details in public threads.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after maintainers have asked to refocus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project. If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers privately with relevant links or screenshots.
